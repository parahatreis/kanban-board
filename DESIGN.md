# Design System

## Overview
OpenCall.ai uses a soft, airy light-mode aesthetic with a healthcare-friendly sense of calm and trust. The interface feels minimal and spacious, with a dark forest-green accent that signals reliability and a lime highlight that adds a subtle, modern AI energy. Overall, the design communicates efficiency, professionalism, and approachability rather than aggressive tech branding.

## Colors
- **Primary/Accent** `#0e2e28`: deep forest green used for headlines, primary buttons, navigation, and brand emphasis.
- **Background** `#f4f9f8`: very light mint-white background that creates the clean, breathable canvas.
- **Text** `#8d9996`: muted gray-green used for supporting copy, metadata, and secondary labels.
- **Highlight Lime** `#cdfa8a`: bright soft lime used for call-to-action text accents, logo mark, and small status highlights.
- **White** `#ffffff`: used for cards, pills, buttons, and cookie surfaces to keep UI crisp.
- **Soft Mint Tint** `#dff7ef`: faint green-tinted surface color visible in overlays and ambient glow effects.
- **Light Gray Border** `#d5e0de`: subtle border color for low-contrast separation and fine lines.
- **Pale Neutral Gray** `#e6eceb`: used in UI dividers, muted controls, and low-emphasis surfaces.
- **Blue Accent** `#4c8bf5`: present in extracted component data, but not prominent in the screenshot; likely unused or legacy styling.

## Typography
- **Headlines**: Archivo, with a strong geometric sans-serif feel. H1 is large and bold at around 51px/700, while other headings stay in Archivo with lighter weights and tight negative letter spacing for a clean, modern look.
- **Body**: Archivo, regular weight, around 16px with generous 24px line height. Body copy is restrained and highly readable, with a calm, product-led tone.
- **Labels/Captions**: Archivo, regular or medium weight, typically smaller and muted in color. Navigation, helper text, and utility labels stay understated rather than visually dominant.

## Components
- **Buttons**: Rounded rectangles with a soft 12px radius and minimal visual chrome. Primary actions use a dark green filled surface with light lime text, while secondary actions often appear as white or transparent pills with subtle borders or low-contrast text.
- **Cards**: White cards with very light inset borders or barely-there shadows. Corners are rounded but not overly pill-like, giving a clean SaaS feel with low elevation.
- **Inputs**: Light, minimal fields with subtle borders and soft backgrounds. Focus states are understated and consistent with the overall quiet interface, avoiding heavy outlines or high-contrast treatment.

## Spacing & Elevation
The layout relies on generous whitespace and large vertical breathing room, especially in the hero area. Spacing feels based on a small, consistent scale, with clear jumps between compact UI elements and larger content sections. Elevation is subtle: shadows are soft, low-opacity, and used mainly to lift floating cards and controls without creating deep depth.

## Do's and Don'ts
- Do: keep layouts spacious and uncluttered with strong negative space.
- Do: use dark forest green for primary emphasis and major text hierarchy.
- Do: pair white surfaces with very subtle borders or soft shadows.
- Do: keep typography large, geometric, and clean, especially for headlines.
- Don't: introduce loud saturated colors that compete with the calm brand palette.
- Don't: use heavy shadows, sharp corners, or dense component stacking.
